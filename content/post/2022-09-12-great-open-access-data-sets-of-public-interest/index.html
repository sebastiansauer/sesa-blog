1 Data

This posts lists some great open-access data sets of public or broad interest. This list is by no means comprehensiv, it’s just a casual curation of interesting sources. Note that this post is not updated, so more frequent releases than documented here are possible.

You’ll find both links to repositories as well as to particular datasets, in no special order.

1.1 Licences

I tried to locate sources with permissible licences only, such as CC BY. But note that some or many of the following data sets may have a non-commerical licence of a completely different licence.

Check for yourself! I do not take any responsibilities.

1.2 General

  • Our World in Data

  • osf.io

  • Figshare

  • Harvard Dataverse

1.3 Environment

  • German Wather Data from the DWD

  • Nasa climeate data such as this data set: Tables of Global and Hemispheric Monthly Means and Zonal Annual Means

  • Data on CO2 and Greenhouse Gas Emissions by Our World in Data

1.4 Health

  • Covid-19 Cases in Germany provided by the RKI.

  • Paper COVIDiSTRESS Global Survey dataset on psychological and behavioural consequences of the COVID-19 outbreak with open data at osf.io.

  • Global Health Data Exchange

  • WHO Global Tuberculosis Programme

  • WHO Global Health Observatory data repository

1.5 Psychology

  • Paper on COVIDiSTRESS Global Survey dataset on psychological and behavioural consequences of the COVID-19 outbreak and the data set at osf.io

  • Graham, Haidt, & Nosek (2009): Liberals and conservatives rely on different sets of moral foundations

  • Brian Nosek Dataverse

  • The Journal of Open Psychology Data (JOPD)

  • Psychology data from the Race Implicit Association Test on the Project Implicit Demo website
